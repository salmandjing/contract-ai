POWER PURCHASE AGREEMENT

This Power Purchase Agreement is entered into as of March 1, 2024 between Solar Genesis LLC (Seller) and Riverside Municipal Power Authority (Buyer).

ARTICLE 1: DEFINITIONS

1.1 Contract Capacity
Contract Capacity means the maximum electrical capacity of 75 megawatts AC.

1.2 Delivery Point
Delivery Point means the interconnection point at the Riverside substation.

1.3 Energy
Energy means three-phase electrical energy measured in megawatt-hours.

1.4 Facility
Facility means the Desert Bloom Solar Facility located in San Bernardino County.

ARTICLE 2: TERM AND EFFECTIVENESS

2.1 Term
This Agreement shall commence on the Commercial Operation Date and continue for twenty (20) years.

2.2 Commercial Operation Date
The Commercial Operation Date shall be no later than December 31, 2025.

ARTICLE 3: FACILITY DEVELOPMENT

3.1 Development Obligations
Seller shall develop, construct, and commission the Facility in accordance with the project schedule.

3.2 Permits and Approvals
Seller shall obtain all necessary permits, licenses, and approvals required for the Facility.

ARTICLE 4: FACILITY OPERATIONS

4.1 Operations and Maintenance
Seller shall operate and maintain the Facility in accordance with good utility practices.

4.2 Availability Standards
Seller shall maintain the Facility to achieve an annual availability of at least 95%.

ARTICLE 5: DELIVERY OF ENERGY

5.1 Delivery Obligation
Seller shall deliver all Energy produced by the Facility to the Delivery Point.

5.2 Scheduling
Seller shall provide day-ahead and hour-ahead forecasts of expected generation.

ARTICLE 6: PRICING AND PAYMENT

6.1 Purchase Price
The purchase price shall be $45 per megawatt-hour for the first ten years.

6.2 Payment Terms
Buyer shall pay all undisputed amounts within thirty (30) days of invoice receipt.

6.3 Price Adjustments
The purchase price shall escalate at 2% annually beginning in year eleven.

ARTICLE 7: REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES

7.1 Seller Representations
Seller represents that it has full authority to enter into this Agreement.

7.2 Buyer Representations
Buyer represents that it has the financial capability to make all payments.

ARTICLE 8: CREDIT AND COLLATERAL

8.1 Credit Requirements
Seller shall maintain a minimum credit rating or provide collateral as specified.

8.2 Security
Buyer may require additional security if Seller's creditworthiness deteriorates.

ARTICLE 9: METERING

9.1 Metering Equipment
All Energy delivered shall be measured by revenue-grade meters.

9.2 Meter Testing
Meters shall be tested annually in accordance with industry standards.

ARTICLE 10: FORCE MAJEURE

10.1 Force Majeure Events
Neither party shall be liable for failure to perform due to force majeure events.

10.2 Notice Requirements
The affected party shall provide prompt written notice of any force majeure event.

ARTICLE 11: EVENTS OF DEFAULT

11.1 Events of Default
An Event of Default includes failure to make payment or material breach of obligations.

11.2 Remedies
Upon an Event of Default, the non-defaulting party may terminate this Agreement.

ARTICLE 12: INDEMNIFICATION

12.1 Seller Indemnification
Seller shall indemnify Buyer from claims arising from Seller's negligence or breach.

12.2 Buyer Indemnification
Buyer shall indemnify Seller from claims arising from Buyer's negligence or breach.

ARTICLE 13: INSURANCE

13.1 Required Coverage
Seller shall maintain commercial general liability insurance of $5,000,000 per occurrence.

13.2 Certificates
Seller shall provide Buyer with certificates of insurance annually.

ARTICLE 14: DISPUTE RESOLUTION

14.1 Negotiation
The parties shall attempt to resolve disputes through good faith negotiations.

14.2 Arbitration
Unresolved disputes shall be submitted to binding arbitration.

ARTICLE 15: MISCELLANEOUS

15.1 Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of California.

15.2 Assignment
Neither party may assign this Agreement without the prior written consent of the other party.

15.3 Notices
All notices shall be in writing and delivered by certified mail or email.

15.4 Entire Agreement
This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement as of the date first written above.
